WELCOME TO

NORDEN TECHLAB (OPC) PRIVATE LIMITED

Founded in 2019 as Norden Technologies, the organization began its journey in the field of material testing as a proprietorship concern. In 2025, it evolved into Norden Techlab, continuing to operate successfully while expanding its capabilities.

The CEO oversees all operations of the testing laboratory, which functions as a core department of NT. Our lab is staffed with experienced and qualified engineers.
